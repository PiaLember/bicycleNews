On the Road:

From the fixed gear fanatic's point of view

18 SEPTEMBER 2023

The whir of the tires against the pavement, the steady rhythm of my legs pumping, the city blurring by in a rush of wind - that's the fixed gear experience. No derailleurs, no fancy gadgets, just pure, unadulterated connection between rider and machine. Some might call it simple, but for us fixed gear fanatics, it's pure freedom.

The Beauty of One Gear

Unlike our multi-geared counterparts, we don't have the luxury of choosing the easiest path. Every climb is a challenge, a test of strength and willpower. But that's what makes it so rewarding. Reaching the top, lungs burning, legs screaming, is a victory unlike any other. It's a constant reminder that you, the rider, are the engine.

Pure Connection

On a fixed gear bike, there's no coasting. You're pedaling constantly, even downhill. This constant engagement creates a unique connection to the road. You feel every bump, every crack, every nuance of the terrain. It's an intimate dance between rider and machine, an experience you just can't get with a traditional geared bike.

Minimalist Magic

Fixed gear bikes are beautiful in their simplicity. No bulky derailleurs, no tangled cables, just clean lines and a raw, mechanical elegance. It's a form that follows function philosophy at its finest. Every component serves a purpose, contributing to the bike's efficiency and aesthetic appeal.

The Zen of Fixed Gear

Riding fixed gear is more than just transportation; it's a meditation on two wheels. The focus required to maintain momentum, the constant rhythm of the pedals, it all creates a sense of zen. It's a way to escape the daily grind, to connect with the city in a whole new way.
